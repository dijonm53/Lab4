◆ read()

encoder_reader.encoder.read ( self)

This method records the total position moved by the motor by recording the difference between the current encoder value and the previous value, and adding it to the total position moved.

In cases of overflow or underflow, the total position will be added or subtracted by the period, respectively.

Returns
the current position read by the encoder to be used for a control loop
